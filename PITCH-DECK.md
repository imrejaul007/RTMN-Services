## Slide 1: The Problem

**Every industry runs on fragmented, siloed software.**

- Hotel has 12 different tools (PMS, POS, Booking Engine, Review Sites, CRM...)
- Restaurant has 8+ systems that don't talk to each other
- Healthcare has legacy systems from 3 different decades
- **$1.5 trillion** spent annually on industry software globally

**The cost of this fragmentation:**
- Data lives in silos — no single view of the customer
- Manual integrations break constantly
- AI/automation can't work across systems
- Every new tool = new vendor, new contract, new training
