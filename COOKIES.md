# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how RTMN Group ("we," "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on the RTMN platform and related websites.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website operators. Cookies may be set by us ("first-party") or by third parties ("third-party cookies").

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the platform to function. They enable authentication, security, and core features. The platform cannot function without them.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `rtmn_session` | Authentication session | Session |
| `rtmn_csrf` | CSRF protection | Session |
| `rtmn_consent` | Stores your cookie preferences | 1 year |
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare bot management | 30 minutes |

**Legal basis:** Legitimate interest (cannot be disabled)

### 2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies
These cookies collect aggregated, anonymous information about how visitors use the platform. We use this data to improve the platform.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguish users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics | Session/state | 2 years |
| `datadog-session-id` | Datadog | Real User Monitoring | Session |

**Legal basis:** Consent (EU), Legitimate interest (US)

### 2.3 Functional Cookies
These cookies remember choices you make (e.g., language, timezone) to provide a personalized experience.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `rtmn_locale` | Language preference | 1 year |
| `rtmn_timezone` | Timezone preference | 1 year |
| `rtmn_theme` | Light/dark mode preference | 1 year |

**Legal basis:** Consent (EU), Legitimate interest (US)

### 2.4 Marketing Cookies
We do **not** currently use marketing or advertising cookies. If we add them in the future, we will:
- Update this policy
- Request explicit consent
- Allow opt-out

**Legal basis:** Consent (always required)

## 3. Your Choices

### 3.1 Cookie Banner
On your first visit, you will see a cookie banner that allows you to:
- **Accept all**
- **Reject non-essential**
- **Customize preferences**

### 3.2 Browser Controls
You can block or delete cookies via your browser settings. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will break platform functionality.

### 3.3 Global Privacy Controls
We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends GPC, we automatically treat it as an opt-out of non-essential cookies.

### 3.4 Do Not Track (DNT)
We honor the DNT signal where technically feasible.

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties (e.g., Google Analytics, Cloudflare, Intercom). These parties may use cookies to:
- Provide their services
- Track usage across websites
- Build profiles for advertising (we do not allow this on our platform)

For details, see the third party's privacy policy:
- [Google Analytics](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)
- [Intercom](https://www.intercom.com/legal/privacy)
