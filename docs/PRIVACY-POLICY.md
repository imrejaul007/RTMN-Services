# RTMN - Privacy Policy
**Effective Date:** June 15, 2026
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Introduction

HOJAI AI Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("RTMN", "we", "us", or "our") operates the RTMN platform (rtmn.io) and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Data:** Name, email address, phone number, company name, job title
- **Payment Data:** Processed by Stripe. RTMN does not store credit card numbers.
- **Content:** Any data you upload to RTMN Industry OS services (e.g., guest profiles, bookings, inventory)
- **Communications:** Emails, support tickets, feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data:** API calls, feature usage, session duration, click patterns
- **Device Data:** Browser type, OS, IP address, device identifiers
- **Logs:** Timestamps, request paths, error messages, performance metrics

| Purpose | Legal Basis |

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|

## 5. Data Sharing

We share data only with:
- **RABTUL Technologies** — Auth, payment, and wallet services you explicitly use
- **Stripe** — Payment processing (subject to [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy))
- **Resend** — Transactional email delivery (subject to their DPA)
- **AWS/Railway** — Cloud infrastructure (data processors under DPA)
- **Legal authorities** — When required by law

No other third-party sharing without your explicit consent.

---

## 6. International Transfers

RTMN processes data primarily in India. If you are an EU/UK resident, data may be transferred outside the EEA/UK under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). By using RTMN, you consent to such transfers.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements
- **Portability** — Receive your data in JSON format
- **Object** — Opt out of marketing at any time
- **Restrict processing** — Limit how we use your data

To exercise any right, email **privacy@rtmn.io**. We respond within **30 days**.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

RTMN is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 9. Security

We implement:
- TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit
- AES-256 encryption at rest (where supported by infrastructure)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- SOC 2 Type II (roadmap: Q4 2026)
