```
Tasks:     Task management, assignments, priorities

Announcements: Company-wide broadcasts
Reporting: Workload, velocity, SLAs
```

#### 1.2 Analytics OS
```
Dashboards:  Real-time KPIs

Forecasting: Revenue, churn, demand
Alerts:      Anomaly detection
```

---

### Phase 2: Intelligence (Week 3-4)

#### 2.1 Market Intelligence OS
```
Trend Detection:    Reddit, TikTok, Google trends
Competitor Analysis: Pricing, positioning, reviews
Demand Prediction:   Search volume, seasonal patterns
Niche Validation:   TAM, SAM, SOM calculation
Keyword Intel:      SEO opportunities
```

#### 2.2 Company Builder Wizard
```
Step 1: Idea Input (text, voice, or AI suggestions)
Step 2: Market Validation (AI analysis)
Step 3: Entity Creation (CorpID, legal setup)
Step 4: Business Type (industry OS selection)
Step 5: Brand Creation (name, logo, identity)
Step 6: Operations Setup (workflows, agents)
Step 7: Go-to-Market (marketing launch)
Step 8: Launch & Monitor
```

---

### Phase 3: Autonomy (Week 5-6)

#### 3.1 AI Company Manager
```
Continuous Monitoring: 24/7 AI oversight
Automated Decisions:    Approvals, escalations
Performance Tuning:    Budget, targeting, pricing
Risk Mitigation:      Fraud, churn, compliance
Growth Optimization:  A/B tests, experiments
```

#### 3.2 Company Portfolio OS
```
Multi-Company:     Manage 100s of companies
Cross-Company:     Shared services, synergies
Performance:       Ranking, comparisons
Resource:          Talent, capital allocation
```
