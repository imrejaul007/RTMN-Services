# [Example Hotel] Increases Guest Satisfaction by 34% with BrandPulse

**Industry:** Hospitality
**Product:** BrandPulse + Hotel OS
**Company Size:** 12-location boutique hotel chain

## The Challenge

Before RTMN, this 12-property boutique hotel chain had no unified way to track and respond to guest feedback. Reviews scattered across Google, TripAdvisor, and Yelp took days to surface and longer to act on.

This resulted in delayed responses to negative reviews, missed service recovery opportunities, and declining TripAdvisor rankings.

---

## The Solution

The hotel chain implemented BrandPulse to aggregate all review sources in real time and Hotel OS to operationalize guest insights across all 12 properties.

Implementation took 5 days. No downtime was experienced during migration.

---

| TripAdvisor ranking (avg) | #47 | #12 | +35 positions |
| Revenue per available room | $142 | $168 | +18% |
