# Sales OS - Enterprise Sales Intelligence Platform

**Version:** 2.0.0  
**Port:** 5055  
**Status:** ✅ **DEPLOYMENT READY**

---

## Overview

Sales OS is the unified sales intelligence platform for the RTMN ecosystem - combining CRM, Customer Success, CPQ, Contract Lifecycle Management, Territory Management, Sales Forecasting, Revenue Intelligence, Partner OS, Sales Enablement, Call/Meeting Intelligence, Workflow Automation, Commission OS, and Subscription Management into one enterprise-grade solution.

---

## Features

### Core CRM
- **Leads Management** - Lead capture, scoring, qualification, conversion
- **Contacts** - Full contact profiles with activity tracking
- **Accounts** - Account hierarchy, segmentation, territory mapping
- **Opportunities** - Full pipeline management with stage tracking
- **Activities** - Tasks, Meetings, Calls, Emails, Notes

### Customer Success
- **Customer Health Scores** - AI-powered health monitoring
- **NPS Surveys** - Net Promoter Score tracking
- **Churn Prediction** - ML-based churn risk detection
- **Renewals** - Renewal tracking and upsell recommendations
- **Onboarding Journeys** - Customer onboarding workflows

### CPQ (Configure-Price-Quote)
- **Products** - Product catalog with pricing
- **Bundles** - Product bundles with discounts
- **Price Books** - Multiple price book support
- **Quotes** - Quote generation with approval workflows
- **Discount Approvals** - Automated discount approval chains

### Contracts & CLM
- **Contract Lifecycle** - Draft → Active → Renewal
- **Version Control** - Track contract changes
- **Amendments** - Contract modification tracking
- **E-Signatures** - Digital signature support

### Territory Management
- **Territory Setup** - Geographic and account-based
- **Quota Allocation** - Fair quota distribution
- **Rep Assignments** - Territory-based assignments

### Sales Forecasting
- **AI Forecasting** - ML-based pipeline predictions
- **Forecast Adjustments** - Manager overrides
- **Pipeline Analysis** - Stage-by-stage analysis

### Revenue Intelligence
- **MRR/ARR Tracking** - Recurring revenue monitoring
- **Cohort Analysis** - Customer cohort performance
- **Revenue Attribution** - Multi-touch attribution

### Partner OS
- **Partner Management** - Partner lifecycle
- **Deal Registration** - Partner deal protection
- **Commission Tracking** - Partner commission calculation

### Sales Enablement
- **Content Library** - Sales collateral
- **Training Modules** - Product and sales training
- **Certifications** - Sales certification tracking
- **Battle Cards** - Competitive intelligence
- **Playbooks** - Sales process playbooks

### Call/Meeting Intelligence
- **Call Recording** - Call capture and storage
- **Transcripts** - Automatic transcription
- **Sentiment Analysis** - AI-powered sentiment
- **Meeting Notes** - Meeting capture and follow-up

### Workflow Automation
- **Automation Rules** - Trigger-based automation
- **Workflow Templates** - Pre-built workflows
- **Execution Logs** - Audit trail

### Commission OS
- **Commission Plans** - Custom commission structures
- **Accelerators** - Tier-based accelerators
- **SPIFFs** - Special incentive programs
- **Payout Tracking** - Commission payout management

### Subscription Management
- **Plan Management** - Subscription tiers
- **Billing Schedules** - Automated billing
- **Plan Changes** - Upgrade/downgrade tracking

## API Endpoints

### Core CRM
- `GET /api/leads` - List all leads
- `POST /api/leads` - Create lead
- `GET /api/leads/:id` - Get lead details
- `PUT /api/leads/:id/qualify` - Qualify lead
- `PUT /api/leads/:id/convert` - Convert to opportunity

### Accounts & Contacts
- `GET /api/accounts` - List accounts
- `POST /api/accounts` - Create account
- `GET /api/contacts` - List contacts
- `POST /api/contacts` - Create contact

### Opportunities
- `GET /api/opportunities` - List opportunities
- `POST /api/opportunities` - Create opportunity
- `PUT /api/opportunities/:id/stage` - Move stage
- `GET /api/pipeline` - Pipeline overview

### Customer Success
- `GET /api/customers` - List customers
- `GET /api/customers/:id/health` - Health score
- `GET /api/nps-surveys` - NPS surveys
- `POST /api/nps-surveys/:id/respond` - Submit NPS

### CPQ
- `GET /api/products` - List products
- `GET /api/bundles` - List bundles
- `POST /api/quotes` - Create quote
- `POST /api/quotes/:id/submit` - Submit for approval

### Activities
- `GET /api/tasks` - List tasks
- `POST /api/tasks` - Create task
- `GET /api/meetings` - List meetings
- `POST /api/meetings` - Schedule meeting
- `GET /api/calls` - List calls
- `POST /api/calls` - Log call
- `GET /api/activities` - Activity feed

### AI & Intelligence
- `GET /api/ai-agents` - List AI agents
- `POST /api/ai-agents/:id/score` - Score lead/deal
- `GET /api/ai-agents/forecast` - Sales forecast

### Industry Bridges
- `GET /api/bridges` - List all industry bridges
- `GET /api/bridges/:industry` - Industry insights
- `GET /api/bridges/:industry/market` - Market data

### RTMN Ecosystem
- `GET /api/rtmn/services` - RTMN service registry
- `GET /api/rtmn/connections` - Service health
- `GET /api/rtmn/customer360/:id` - Customer 360
- `POST /api/rtmn/events` - Publish event

### REZ-SalesMind
- `GET /api/salesmind/agents` - SalesMind agents
- `POST /api/salesmind/leads/enrich` - Enrich lead
- `GET /api/salesmind/customer360/:id` - Customer 360
- `GET /api/salesmind/churn/:id` - Churn prediction
- `POST /api/salesmind/next-action` - Get next action
