# Education OS

Industry-specific digital twin for education management.

## Port

**5060** - Education OS

## API

- `GET/POST /api/courses` - Course management
- `GET/POST /api/students` - Student management
- `GET/POST /api/instructors` - Instructor management
- `GET/POST /api/enrollments` - Enrollment management
- `GET/POST /api/assignments` - Assignment management
- `GET/POST /api/grades` - Grade management
- `GET /api/analytics` - Dashboard
