## What is hojai.ai?

**hojai.ai** is an AI-native company building platform that lets founders launch a fully autonomous company in 30 minutes — not just a website, not just an AI chatbot, but an entire **operational company with 6 AI employees, real revenue channels, and federation-ready infrastructure**.

Unlike WordPress/Webflow (content), Shopify (storefront), Vercel (deploy), or Salesforce (CRM), hojai.ai is the **company-as-a-service**: you describe what you want to build, and the platform assembles, deploys, and operates the company for you.

## Key Differentiators

### 1. Autonomous Commerce Score (ACS)

**What:** A trust + reputation score for every company in the federation (0-1000, similar to credit scores).

**Why it matters:** Buyers know who to trust before they transact. Sellers know which buyers are reliable.

**How:** Composite of transaction history, response time, dispute rate, insurance, verification level, ACI (Autonomous Commerce Index).

### 2. AI Employees (not AI assistants)

**What:** 6 pre-built AI agents that actually DO the work — sales, support, procurement, finance, operations, marketing.

**Why it matters:** Assistants answer questions. Agents close deals, process orders, manage suppliers.

**How:** Built on SUTAR (AI employee framework) + REZ Intelligence for context + TwinOS for memory.

### 3. Federation Network (not isolated)

**What:** Every hojai.ai company is part of a network of 100+ companies (Nexhas) that discover each other, transact with each other, and build trust through shared reputation.

**Why it matters:** No cold start — your first customer could be a Nexha 3 hops away with a perfect ACS.

**How:** FederationOS (port 4273) + DiscoveryOS (port 4272) + ReputationOS (port 4271) + GlobalDirectory (port 4276).

### 4. REZ Coin (not just Stripe)

**What:** A loyalty + utility token that earns on every transaction and can be spent across the federation.

**Why it matters:** Repeat customers, network loyalty, zero-fee internal transactions.

**How:** 1 REZ = 1 INR. Minted on commission. Burned on spend. 2%/year inactive decay.
