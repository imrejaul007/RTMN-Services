## 🗺️ Where We're Going

### 🆕 Mass Adoption Layer (Q3 2026, 8 weeks — **BUILD FIRST**)

> **The most important phase. Make RAZO usable by anyone within 30 seconds.**

**The Goal:** A daily-wage worker, a grandmother, a shopkeeper, and a startup founder should all feel the product made their lives easier within the first day.

**12 Consumer-First Features:**

#### 🎯 P0 — Build First (8 features)
1. **✨ Help Me Button** — One tap, RAZO figures out everything
2. **📱 My Mom Mode** — 8 big buttons, no jargon, no settings
3. **🆘 Emotion Buttons** — Calm/Say Nice/What Reply/Quick
4. **🎤 Voice-First** — Speak instead of type, wake word "Hey RAZO"
5. **🌍 Regional Languages** — Hinglish, Assamese, Bengali, Arabic, Hindi, English
6. **👨‍👩‍👧 Family Quick Reply** — Auto context-aware replies for family
7. **🔮 Auto Life Assistant** — Proactive suggestions (flight/cab/rain/birthday)
8. **🗣️ Talk to Phone** — Voice → Intent → Execution end-to-end
9. **💰 Pay Anyone** — Voice/Photo/Contact money transfer

#### 🎁 P1 — Build Second (3 features)
10. **🎁 Family Help** — Birthday/anniversary/health auto-care
11. **🛒 Order Usual** — One-tap repeat orders
12. **🎊 Festival Greetings** — Eid/Diwali/Bihu auto-suggestions

**The 3 Golden Rules (Non-Negotiable):**
1. Never make users type if tapping is possible.
2. Never make users think if RAZO can infer.
3. Never make users open another app if RAZO can execute.

**The Grandma Test:** Every feature must be usable by a 65-year-old without help.

**Exit Criteria:** 70% of users complete valuable action in 30s, NPS > 50, 7-day retention > 60%

#### 🌟 P1 — Power Layer (7 features)
