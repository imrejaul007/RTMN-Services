## STOP DOING / START DOING v3.0

### STOP DOING

- ❌ Building 24 separate copilots
- ❌ Treating Genie as Business Copilot
- ❌ Treating BOA as user-facing product
- ❌ Confusing BOA (strategic) with SUTAR (execution)
- ❌ Fragmenting twins without hierarchy
- ❌ Building BOA before Business Copilot
- ❌ Building SUTAR before foundation

### START DOING

- ✅ Building four distinct operating systems (Genie, Business Copilot, BOA, SUTAR)
- ✅ Genie = Personal (stays personal)
- ✅ Business Copilot = Business (user-facing, ask/analyze/recommend/simulate/execute)
- ✅ BOA = Executive backend (what should happen?)
- ✅ SUTAR = Execution frontend (how should it happen?)
- ✅ Explicit Twin Hierarchy (Human, Business, Organization, Asset, Market, Agent, Relationship)
- ✅ Foundation first (Identity, Memory, Twin)
- ✅ Business Copilot before BOA (users talk to Copilot first)
