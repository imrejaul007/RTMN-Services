## WHAT IS HOJAI SALON & SPA OS?

HOJAI Salon & Spa OS is an AI-powered salon management system that helps salons, spas, and beauty centers manage appointments, staff, inventory, and customer relationships with personalized beauty recommendations.

**Target Customers:**
- Salons (hair, beauty)
- Spas & wellness centers
- Nail salons
- Barber shops
- Multi-service beauty centers
