## WHAT IS HOJAI RETAIL OS?

HOJAI Retail OS is an AI-powered retail management system for stores, shops, and retail chains. It combines POS, inventory management, demand forecasting, and customer analytics into one platform.

**Target Customers:**
- Fashion boutiques
- Electronics stores
- Grocery stores
- Convenience stores
- Multi-location retail chains
