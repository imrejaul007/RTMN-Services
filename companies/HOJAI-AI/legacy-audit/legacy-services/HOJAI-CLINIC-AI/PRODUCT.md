## WHAT IS HOJAI CLINIC AI?

HOJAI Clinic AI is an AI-powered healthcare operating system for clinics, hospitals, and healthcare providers. It combines AI employees, voice interfaces, and healthcare intelligence into one platform.

**Target Customers:**
- Single-doctor clinics
- Multi-specialty clinics
- Dental practices
- Veterinary clinics
- Diagnostic centers

---

## PRODUCTS

### 1. Hojai Clinic AI (Core)

**AI Employees:**
- AI Receptionist - Answers calls, books appointments
- Appointment AI - Schedules, reschedules, cancels
- Follow-up AI - Post-visit follow-ups, reminders
- WhatsApp AI - Patient queries via WhatsApp
- Nurse Assistant - Vitals collection, triage

**Services:**
- Patient Registration
- Appointment Booking
- Teleconsultation
- Health Records
- Prescription Management
- Lab Integration
- Payment Collection

### 2. Hojai Hospital AI

**AI Employees:**
- Patient Journey AI - Tracks patient flow
- Care Coordinator AI - Manages care plans
- Discharge AI - Handles discharge process
- Revenue AI - Billing and collections

**Services:**
- Bed Management
- OT Scheduling
- IP Management
- Estate Management

### 3. Hojai Diagnostic AI

**AI Employees:**
- Report AI - Interprets lab/radiology reports
- Follow-up AI - Result communication
- Patient Retention AI - Recall management

**Services:**
- Lab Orders
- Sample Collection
- Report Generation
- Result Communication
