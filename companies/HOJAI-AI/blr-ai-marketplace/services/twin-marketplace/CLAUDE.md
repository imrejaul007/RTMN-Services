# Twin Marketplace

**Port:** 4146
**Type:** BLR AI Marketplace Service (Twin Listings)
**Package:** `@hojai/blr-twin-marketplace`

## Overview

Buy and sell pre-built digital twins. Publishers list twin templates (Customer Twin, Order Twin, Hotel Twin, etc.); consumers install them into their workspace. The marketplace wraps the TwinOS Hub (4705) registry with discovery, ratings, and payment.
