### Quick Start

1. **Sign Up** at `/login`
2. **Complete Setup** at `/dashboard/settings`
3. **Connect Stripe** to receive payments
4. **Publish Listing** at `/dashboard/publish`
5. **Wait for Approval** (24-48 hours)
