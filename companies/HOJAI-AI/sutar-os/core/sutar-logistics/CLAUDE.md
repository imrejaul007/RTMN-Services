# SUTAR Logistics

**Port:** 4285
**Type:** SUTAR OS Core (Network Service)
**Package:** `sutar-logistics`

## Overview

SUTAR Logistics & Shipping Service (Phase C.2 of the Nexha network). Provides shipping quotes, label generation, tracking, and delivery status across multiple carriers. Pairs with the Supplier Registry (4280) for fulfillment and Trade Finance (4287) for COD/escrow.
