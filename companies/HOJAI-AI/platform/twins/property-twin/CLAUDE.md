# Property Twin

**Port:** TBD (default 3000)
**Type:** Platform Twin Service (Real Estate)
**Package:** `@rtmn/property-twin`

## Overview

Digital twin for real estate properties. Tracks valuation, listing history, maintenance, occupancy, comparable sales, and tenant information. Used by RealEstate OS (5230) and the Area Twin (4964) for location context.
