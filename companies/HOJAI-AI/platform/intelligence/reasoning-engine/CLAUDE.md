# Reasoning Engine

**Port:** 4795
**Type:** Platform Intelligence Service (LLM Reasoning)
**Package:** `@hojai/reasoning-engine`

## Overview

Multi-step planning and tool execution for complex Genie requests. Decomposes a user query into sub-tasks, selects tools, executes in order, and synthesizes a final answer. Pairs with Intent Engine (4792) and uses MemoryOS for context.
