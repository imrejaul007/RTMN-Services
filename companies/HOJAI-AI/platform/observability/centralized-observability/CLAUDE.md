# Centralized Observability

**Port:** 4153
**Type:** Platform Observability Service (Hub)
**Package:** `@hojai/centralized-observability`

## Overview

Collects metrics, traces, and logs from all HOJAI AI and RTMN services into one queryable hub. Foundation for SRE, alerting, and dashboards. Pairs with Observability APIs (4172) for public query access.
