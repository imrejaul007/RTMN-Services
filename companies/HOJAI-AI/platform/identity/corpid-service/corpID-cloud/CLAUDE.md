# CorpID Cloud

**Port:** 4702
**Type:** Platform Identity Service (Enterprise Identity)
**Package:** `@rtmn/corpid-cloud`

## Overview

Enterprise Identity Platform — multi-tenant CorpID for organizations. Issues tenants, manages SSO, roles, groups, and federates identity to consumer-facing CorpID (4702). This is the **enterprise** surface; the canonical CorpID lives one level up at `platform/identity/corpid-service/`.
