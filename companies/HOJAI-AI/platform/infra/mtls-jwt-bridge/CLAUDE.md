# Service-to-Service Auth Bridge (mTLS-JWT Bridge)

**Port:** 4779
**Type:** Platform Infra Service (Security)
**Package:** `@hojai/mtls-jwt-bridge`

## Overview

JWT + signed token issuance, rotation, and verification for inter-service communication. An alternative to mTLS that works without production cert infrastructure. Pairs with CorpID (4702) for principal identity.
