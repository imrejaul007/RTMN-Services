## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch plans?**
A: Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What counts as a "review"?**
A: Each review from a source (Google, Yelp, etc.) is one review. Aggregated scores do not count.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my limit?**
A: We'll notify you at 80% and 100%. Overages are billed at $0.01/review (BrandPulse) or $0.0001/API call (Industry OS). You can also upgrade or set hard caps.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes — 14-day free trial on Professional plans. No credit card required.

**Q: How does annual billing work?**
A: Pay 12 months upfront, save 20%. Invoiced annually. Refundable within 30 days.

**Q: Do you offer nonprofit discounts?**
A: Yes — 50% off for registered nonprofits. Contact nonprofit@rtmn.com.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH, wire transfer, and invoicing (Enterprise only).

**Q: How do I cancel?**
A: Cancel from Settings → Billing at any time. No cancellation fees. You retain access until the end of your billing period.
