## Proposal Checklist

Before sending:
- [ ] Customized for this specific client (no generic language)
- [ ] All numbers are accurate (pricing, timelines, metrics)
- [ ] Client's challenges are specifically named
- [ ] ROI calculation is reasonable and defensible
- [ ] Legal has reviewed if new terms are proposed
- [ ] Proposal number assigned and tracked
- [ ] Valid-until date is 30 days out
- [ ] PDF version is attached
- [ ] Follow-up call scheduled within 48 hours of sending
