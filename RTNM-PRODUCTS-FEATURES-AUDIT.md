## Issue → Resolution Flow Features

### Step-by-Step Flow

```
1. ISSUE DETECTED
   ├── WhatsApp/Email/Chat/Phone/Social
   └── AI parses intent

2. AI ANALYSIS
   ├── Intent detection
   ├── Sentiment analysis
   ├── Entity extraction
   └── Policy matching

3. CUSTOMER TWIN LOADED
   ├── Trust Score
   ├── Order history
   ├── Payment history
   ├── Support history
   └── VIP tier

4. DECISION MADE
   ├── Auto-approve (VIP + Trust 90%+)
   ├── Human review (Trust < 50%)
   └── Manager escalation (Amount > ₹10,000)

5. REFUND/RESOLUTION
   ├── Refund processed
   ├── Ticket resolved
   └── Customer notified

6. TWINS UPDATED
   ├── Customer Twin
   ├── Payment Twin
   └── Journey Twin

7. OUTCOME TRACKED
   ├── Revenue impact
   ├── Agent time saved
   └── CSAT impact

8. LEARNED
   ├── KB updated
   ├── Policy improved
   └── AI model tuned
```
