## 🎫 How to Open a Ticket

**Include the following in your request:**

1. **Account ID / Workspace ID**
2. **Product** (e.g., BrandPulse, Hotel OS)
3. **Severity** (see below)
4. **Description** of the issue
5. **Steps to reproduce** (if applicable)
6. **Screenshots / logs** (if applicable)

## 🚨 Severity Levels

| Severity | Definition | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------|------------|----------------|-------------------|
| **P1 — Critical** | Production down, data loss, security incident | < 15 min | < 4 hours |
| **P2 — High** | Major feature broken, no workaround | < 1 hour | < 8 hours |
| **P3 — Medium** | Minor feature issue, workaround exists | < 4 hours | < 3 business days |
| **P4 — Low** | Cosmetic, feature request | < 1 business day | < 10 business days |
