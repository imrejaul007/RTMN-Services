## Payment Methods

| Method | Available For | Processing Time |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|

## Billing Cycles

- **Monthly:** Billed on the same day each month
- **Annual:** Billed once per year; 20% savings vs monthly

---

## Invoices

Invoices are generated automatically and sent to your billing email:
- **PDF invoice** — attached to billing email
- **Download** — Settings → Billing → Invoice History
- **Tax** — Invoices include applicable tax (VAT, GST, etc.) based on your billing address

---

## Upgrades & Downgrades

### Upgrades
Take effect **immediately**. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing period.

**Example:** You're on Starter ($99/mo) on day 15 of your billing cycle. You upgrade to Professional ($299/mo). You pay: ($299 - $99) × (15 remaining days / 30 days) = **$100**.

### Downgrades
Take effect at the **next billing cycle**. No refund for the current period.

---

## Failed Payments

1. **First failure:** Email notification with retry in 3 days
2. **Second failure:** Email notification with retry in 3 days
3. **Third failure:** Account suspended, 7-day grace period
4. **After grace period:** Account terminated, data retained 30 days

To update your payment method: Settings → Billing → Payment Method

---

## Refunds

- **Monthly plans:** No refunds after billing date
- **Annual plans:** Refundable within 30 days of purchase
- **Add-ons:** No refunds for one-time add-ons
- **Enterprise contracts:** As specified in MSA

To request a refund: billing@rtmn.com

---

## Usage-Based Billing

For plans with usage limits (API calls, reviews, etc.):

- **Soft limit (80%):** Email notification
- **Hard limit (100%):** You can still use the service; overages billed at overage rate
- **Or set hard cap:** No overage billing; service pauses at limit

Current usage: Settings → Billing → Usage

---

## Tax

We collect tax based on your billing address:
- **US:** Sales tax where applicable
- **EU:** VAT (provide EU VAT number to reverse charge)
- **UK:** VAT (provide UK VAT number to reverse charge)
- **Other countries:** As required by local law

To update your tax ID: Settings → Billing → Tax Information

## Credits

- **Promotional credits:** Applied automatically to future invoices
- **Service credits:** Issued for SLA violations; applied to future invoices
- Credits expire after 12 months

## Enterprise Invoicing

For Enterprise customers:
- **Net 30** payment terms (default)
- **Purchase orders** accepted
- **Multi-entity billing:** Single invoice for multiple subsidiaries
- **Custom billing address** per entity
- **PO number** field on invoice
- **Month-end close** accommodated

Contact billing@rtmn.com to set up invoicing.
