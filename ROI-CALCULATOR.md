## BrandPulse ROI Framework

### Revenue Impact

| Driver | Calculation | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Review response rate** | (Response rate improvement × Total reviews × Win-back rate × Avg order value) | (40% → 60% × 1,000 reviews × 15% × $50) = **$1,500/month** |
| **Sentiment improvement** | (Sentiment lift × Total reviews × Price premium) | (0.3 points × 1,000 reviews × $2 premium) = **$2,000/month** |
| **TripAdvisor ranking** | (Ranking improvement × Traffic increase × Conversion rate × AOV) | (35 positions × 20% traffic × 3% × $50) = **$2,100/month** |
| **Repeat customer rate** | (Repeat rate improvement × Total customers × AOV × Visits/year) | (5% × 500 × $50 × 3) = **$3,750/month** |

### Cost Savings

| Driver | Calculation | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Manual review tracking** | (Hours saved × Fully-loaded labor cost) | (5 hrs/week × 52 × $50/hr) = **$13,000/year** |
| **Reduced customer churn** | (Churn reduction × Customer count × LTV) | (3% × 500 × $200) = **$3,000/month** |
| **Faster complaint resolution** | (Time saved × Resolution rate × Avg ticket value) | (3 days × 40% × $200) = **$9,600/year** |
| **Reduced公关 crisis** | (Crisis events prevented × Avg crisis cost) | (2 × $5,000) = **$10,000/year** |

## Talking Points

**For skeptical prospects:**

> "A single negative review on TripAdvisor costs the average hotel $5,000 in lost revenue. BrandPulse Professional costs $299/month. That's one recovered negative review every 2 months to break even."

**For operations-focused prospects:**

> "Your team spends 5+ hours a week manually tracking reviews across 4 sites. BrandPulse automates that entirely. At $50/hour, that's $13,000/year in labor savings — more than 4x the Professional plan cost."

**For marketing-focused prospects:**

> "The average hotel that improves from 3.8 to 4.2 stars sees a 15% increase in bookings. With 100 reviews/month, that's 1,200 impressions per month. At a 3% conversion rate and $150 average booking, that's $5,400/month in additional revenue."
