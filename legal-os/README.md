# Legal OS

Industry-specific digital twin for legal services management.

## Port

**5035** - Legal OS

## API

- `GET/POST /api/clients` - Client management
- `GET/POST /api/cases` - Case management
- `GET/POST /api/lawyers` - Lawyer management
- `GET/POST /api/documents` - Document management
- `GET/POST /api/appointments` - Appointment scheduling
- `GET/POST /api/invoices` - Invoice management
- `GET /api/analytics` - Dashboard
