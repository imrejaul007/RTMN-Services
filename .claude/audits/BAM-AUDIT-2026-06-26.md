**CLAUDE.md claims:**
> "Next.js Storefront (the user-facing web app at the BLR root)"

**Reality:**
- `package.json` has Next.js 14 dependencies
- No `app/` directory

- No pages
- No API routes

**This is a critical blocker.** BAM has no UI.

## 12. Conclusion

BAM has a solid **foundation** (marketplace-listings, discovery-engine, twin-marketplace) but is **far from the canonical vision** of "the world's largest marketplace for AI-native business assets."

The gap is primarily in:
1. **No frontend** — can't browse or buy anything
2. **No payments** — can't monetize
3. **No Hub integration** — not accessible from RTMN ecosystem
4. **Missing 27+ categories** — only 8 of 35+ implemented
5. **No AI recommender** — just basic keyword search
6. **No Business Capability Packs** — the killer feature

**The spec says "70% built." The reality is closer to 12%.**
