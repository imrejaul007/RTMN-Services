## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Legal AI Assistant is an AI-powered legal research, document drafting, and case management platform for lawyers, businesses, and individuals.

**Why Now:**
- 2M+ legal matters pending in Indian courts
- 50,000+ lawyers in India
- 10M+ legal documents generated annually
- 80% of legal time spent on research
- Growing demand for legaltech

### Legal Research

2. Precedent Finder - Similar cases
3. Citation Checker - Verifying citations
4. Statute Research - Act sections
5. Judgment Analyzer - Outcome prediction

### Document Drafting
1. Contract Templates - 100+ templates
2. Clause Library - Reusable clauses
3. Clause Analyzer - Risk assessment
4. NDA Generator - Quick NDAs

### Court Integration
