## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Credit Score OS is an AI-powered credit scoring and financial wellness platform. It provides comprehensive credit monitoring, score improvement guidance, and loan matching services.

**Why Now:**
- 500M+ credit-active Indians
- Only 20% have formal credit access
- Digital lending growing 40% annually
- Rising awareness of credit health
- Regulatory push for credit inclusion
