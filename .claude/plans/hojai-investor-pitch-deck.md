## Pitch Deck Anti-Patterns to Avoid

| Anti-pattern | Why it kills the pitch |
|---|---|
| Too many slides | 10 max. More = you don't know what's important. |
| Too much text | Slides are visual aids. You tell the story. |
| Vague market numbers | VCs will Google your TAM. Don't make it up. |
| Unrealistic projections | Conservative builds trust. Ambitious destroys it. |
| No "why now" | VCs need urgency. Without it, they'll pass. |
| Weak team slide | This is where deals die. Make it strong. |
| No clear ask | "What are you raising?" must be unambiguous. |
| Too much tech jargon | VCs aren't all technical. Speak outcomes. |
| No competitive analysis | They WILL ask "what about X?" Be ready. |
| No demo | If you have a product, SHOW IT. Always. |
