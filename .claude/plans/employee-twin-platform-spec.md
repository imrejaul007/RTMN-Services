# Employee Twin Platform — Product Specification

**Version:** 1.0 | **Date:** June 28, 2026 | **Product:** P0 | **Build:** ₹40L / 6 weeks | **ARR:** ₹5.0Cr

---

## 1. Concept & Vision

Every employee has a digital twin that knows their skills, knowledge, work patterns, goals, and career trajectory — boosting productivity by pairing humans with AI assistants.

**RTMN Fit:** Uses existing employee-twin, MemoryOS, SkillOS, CorpID, SUTAR. Existing: 95%.

---

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1 Twin Architecture (P0)
- Skills, knowledge, experiences
- Work patterns (peak hours, communication style)
- Goals & career aspirations
- Performance tracking

### 2.2 AI Pairing (P0)
- AI learns work patterns
- Anticipates needs proactively
- Automates repetitive tasks

### 2.3 Knowledge Capture (P0)
- Meeting summaries
- Decision documentation

### 2.4 Skills Tracking (P1)
- Real-time skill mapping
- Skill gap analysis
