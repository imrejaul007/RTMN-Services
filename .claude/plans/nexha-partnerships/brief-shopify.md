## TL;DR

Shopify powers **5 million merchants**. None of them have AI to source products, negotiate with suppliers, and manage procurement.

## Why Shopify Needs Nexha

Shopify is **excellent at D2C sales** (storefront, payments, fulfillment). Shopify is **bad at B2B procurement** — the other half of merchant ops.

Today, Shopify merchants:
- ❌ Email 50 suppliers for quotes manually
- ❌ Negotiate prices in spreadsheets
- ❌ Sign POs in Word docs
- ❌ Pay via wire transfer
- ❌ Track shipments in 5 different carrier portals

**Nexha automates all of this with AI.**

Shopify has been building its own AI (Shopify Magic, Sidekick). They might want to build this themselves.

**Mitigation:**
- Position as **complementary** — Nexha handles supplier-side, Shopify handles storefront-side
- Emphasize **vendor neutrality** — merchants don't want lock-in
- Offer **co-development** rather than partnership
